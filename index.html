Who we are

We are a team of award winning, Germany based designers, that are passionate about creating unique & inspiring spaces. Our motivation for any project is to give the client something that they are proud to shout about.This means that we bring head-turning design to spaces, places, people and brands. It’s what we like doing best and it’s what we’re best at.

Elite Interiors has experience in Restaurant Design, Retail Design, Exhibition Design, Night Club Design, Cafe Design, Office Design, Bathroom Design, Residential Design and Health Care Interior.

We love what we do and that’s why, for us, it’s not just about the design, it's about the journey.
